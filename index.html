Campus Life

Science Lab

In the Lab Wing of the School, housed separately on Levels 2,3and 4, are the Physics, Chemistry and Biology Labs. They are equipped with the latest and syllabus- prescribed equipment, carefully and diligently taught by competent teachers.

school library

The Library is housed on Level 4 of the newly constructed Administrative Wing. With a wide spectrum of books, magazines and Encyclopaedias to choose from, the students and staff are engaged in a healthy exercise of the mind. Plans are afoot to completely computerise the inventory of the Library.
